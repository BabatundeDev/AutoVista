Automotive Evolution

Witness the milestones that shaped the automotive industry and continue to drive innovation forward.

1886

Birth of the Automobile

Karl Benz patents the first practical automobile, the Benz Patent-Motorwagen, marking the beginning of the automotive era.

1908

Mass Production Era

Henry Ford introduces the Model T and assembly line production, making automobiles accessible to the masses.

1970s

Safety Revolution

Introduction of airbags, anti-lock braking systems, and comprehensive safety regulations transform automotive design priorities.

Electric Revolution

The automotive industry embraces electrification, autonomous driving, and sustainable mobility solutions for a cleaner future.
